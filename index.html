I work on computer control agents and on collaborative learning with LLMs at Google DeepMind. Previously, I wrote my master's thesis on federated learning, spent a few wonderful months at Mozilla working on a Firefox implementation, and then continued to work on federated learning at Google Research for a few years. Prior to that, I also interned on Differential Privacy and recommender systems at Mozilla, and on NLP at Amazon.

I occasionally like to develop apps, such as a clipboard manager or a Hacker News client. During high school, I published several popular JavaScript libraries.
